Our Brands

Edusignis

Edusignis is an IBM partner, offering specialized technical training to help cliënts acquire, maintain and optimize their skills. Our training ranges from administrator to developer and end-user skills and is delivered by certified BI trainers who have a solid foundation of theory and hands-on experience in designing and developing solutions

Datasignis

Datasignis provides solutions that support whichever tools are in place, with data-driven activation, and ensuring that our client’s data is at the right level before they make a move on the technical considerations.

Intellisignis

At Intellisignis, we believe in answering real business questions with our extensive experience in developing and implementing Business Analytics Solutions. We apply and develop methodologies and standards focused on our individual customers.

Talentsignis

Talentsignis is a specialist permanent and contract placement consultancy providing some of the top IT jobs in the country.

Caresignis

Caresignis is the corporate social investment division of Praesignis. We address current social issues within our communities and partner with charities and NPO’s, providing resources, expertise, time as well as funding where applicable.
